Bandstand through the years

Bandstand set foot into the delhi music circuit in the year 1991 with its first event being organised by Mr Stanley Vaz, This event hosted popular bands like Parikrama who played to adorn the stage with mesmerizing tunes. Down through the timeline, in 2014-15, Bandstand also bacame a part of October Fest held in Goa and further organised it's winter edition in Goa itself. Some of the best bands in Goa lit the stage of Bandstand with fires of perfection.
