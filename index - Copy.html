Bandstand is aimed at giving a stage to upcoming artists brimming with potential. Bandstand identifies and helps artists showcase their potential through means of concerts, music festivals, music meets and competitions. It unites talented individuals who are interested in successfully being able to reach out a larger audience with their extraordinary skillset, technique and musicality, Bandstand also helps in fundraising for various charities through numerous events.
